## EXACT USE CASES — MICRO DRONE SWARMS

### CONSTRUCTION
- **Shelter printing:** Nano swarms 3D print foundations + concrete superstructure from local aggregate. ScanDrones verify geometry in real-time (5mm accuracy, per Imperial College Aerial-AM research). A 4-week shelter becomes a 48-hour print.
- **Infrastructure:** Bridges, retaining walls, water cisterns, root cellars — printed in place without scaffolding or cranes.
- **Repair:** Swarms detect cracks via thermal/sonar, print patching compound directly onto damage.
- **Scale:** 10 drones = home. 200 = community hall. 2000 = dam.

### AGRICULTURE
- **Pollination:** Where bees are compromised, micro drones replicate flight patterns for targeted food forest pollination.
- **Soil scanning:** Each drone carries a spectrometer, mapping nutrients across the entire food forest in one afternoon. Data feeds the governance graph directly.
- **Pest management:** Nanoscale targeted spray, replacing broad-spectrum chemicals.

### WATER
- **Stream cleanup:** Oil-absorbing nano drones patrol surfaces, collecting contaminants into filter bags.
- **Irrigation:** Swarms print clay pipe networks from local soil, installing drip irrigation without trenching.
- **Rainfall enhancement:** Conductive nano drones seeded into clouds to trigger precipitation in drought-prone areas.

### ENERGY
- **Solar installation:** Drones lift and position panels, weld connections, calibrate angle.
- **Micro-hydro turbine cleaning:** Nanoscale scrapers remove bio-fouling in situ.
- **Mesh line maintenance:** Wire inspection and splice repair without bucket trucks.

### GOVERNANCE
- **Resource mapping:** Before any council decision, drones produce 3D terrain + soil + water table + vegetation survey. Proposals start with ground truth.
- **Compliance verification:** Drones verify setback/buffer compliance and report to governance graph.
- **Emergency:** Medical supply delivery, evacuation routing, damage assessment within minutes.

### MANUFACTURING (THE FRACTAL LOOP)
- **Dark factory replication:** The factory that builds drones prints components for the next factory.
- **Month 1:** Swarm prints simple structures. Metal recycler processes salvage into drone parts. Drones print a second swarm.
- **Month 6:** Two communities, each with a dark factory producing its own replacement parts.
- **Month 18:** Eight communities. Specialized swarms for agriculture, water, energy, construction.
- **Month 36:** Fractal replication. Every new community starts with a dark factory the previous swarm printed.

### DEFENSE / BOUNDARY
- **Firebreak:** Rapid-response swarms cut firebreaks by depositing fire-resistant foam barriers.
- **Flood defense:** Sand bag printing swarms work faster than human sandbag lines.
- **Invasive species:** Nanoscan drones identify and mark invasive plants for removal crews.

## THE MONEY-FREE ASSERTION

If money is deprecated with severe punishment:
- Value flows through contribution, not purchase
- Reputation replaces currency (governance graph tracks it)
- Resources flow toward need, not profit
- Drone swarms allocated by council, not by who can pay

Scarcity becomes a local temporary condition, not a systemic one.
