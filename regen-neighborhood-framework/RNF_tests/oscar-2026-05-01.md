## Vision

**One-liner:** Re-develop society

**What you're building:**
A regenerative neighborhood with resilient systems for housing, water, food, energy, economy, ecology, human health & connection. Designed as a **destination village** with a significant visitor economy.

**For whom:**
Multigenerational community — singles and families — regenerative mindsets, growth-minded, emotionally intelligent humans who want to collaborate to make awesome businesses, art, and experiences.

**Core values:**
- Land held in common (never privatized)
- Decisions follow governance layers (sociocracy + meritocracy + democracy + AI advisor)
- Zero waste — solutions for all streams
- Universal participation — everyone can earn decision-making abilities
- Contributors rewarded by income that covers costs + generates income
- Nature as partner, included in income share

**Membership categories:**

| Category | Entry Path | Commitment | Voting Rights |
|----------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Applicant** | Application + 30-min conversation | None — exploring | None |
| **Visitor** | Site tour (virtual or in-person) | None | None |
| **Resident (Trial)** | 13-week onboarding, new cohorts every 2 weeks | Month-to-month ($1.5K–$3.5K/mo) | None until graduation |
| **Member (Full)** | Graduated from onboarding, community vote | Pioneer or Founder investment | Full voting rights |
| **Founding Member** | Joined pre-Phase 2 | Pioneer investment | Full voting + founding decisions |

**Fractal house ownership:** Multiple members per lot are allowed — each with individual membership. No cap on total membership count.

**Membership scope:** All people accessing community amenities require membership + onboarding — residents, day visitors, retreat guests alike.

**Path to Membership:**
1. **Apply** — Submit interest form
2. **Connect** — 30-minute conversation with community coordinator
3. **Visit** — Virtual or in-person site tour
4. **Join cohort** — New cohorts begin every 2 weeks (waitlist when full)
5. **Onboard** — Complete 13-week program
6. **Graduate** — Community vote
7. **Activate** — Full membership begins

**Graduation criteria:**
- Completed all 13 weeks of onboarding
- Demonstrated alignment with community values and invariants
- Passed community vote (existing members)
- Work-to-earn commitment confirmed

**Graduation retry:** Mentor/tutor assigned on first fail. Second fail = continued mentorship + third attempt. Third attempt requires full community approval.

**Exit pathways:**
- Resident tier: 30-day notice, month-to-month
- Pioneer/Founders: Exit clause in membership agreement (TBD)
- Community vote can revoke membership for invariant violations

**Join process:** Apply → 30-min conversation → tour → choose tier → begin onboarding (cohorts every 2 weeks)

## Governance Stack

Multi-layer system:
1. **Sociocracy (circles/departments)** — operations, domain autonomy
2. **Meritocracy** — expert decisions
3. **Democracy** — one person one vote, strategic/veto layer
4. **Benevolent AI advisor** — decision engine trained on documented values, principles, rules, and decisions; activates for verification when human layers need it

**Verification rule:** AI + 3 layers of human voting confirm when necessary.

**RCOS 7-Layer framework:**
- Layer 0: Identity & Purpose
- Layer 1: Membership
- Layer 2: Governance
- Layer 3: Economy & Resources
- Layer 4: Conflict & Accountability
- Layer 5: Operations & Coordination
- Layer 6: Evolution
