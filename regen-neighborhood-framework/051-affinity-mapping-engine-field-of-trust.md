# Affinity Mapping Engine and Field of Trust

Affinity Mapping Engine stands for Affinity Mapping Engine.
Field of Trust stands for Field of Trust.
Both are concepts from the Mythogen framework.

## Affinity Mapping Engine

Affinity means natural attraction to work.

Affinity means shared purpose without coercion.

Affinity mapping finds patterns in group affinity.

It finds who wants to work on what.

It finds who works well with whom.

The engine listens to group signals.

It does not extract data.

It observes patterns.

It surfaces affinities.

It does not assign.

## Field of Trust

Trust emerges from repeated interaction.

Trust follows a formula.

Trust equals evidence divided by claims.

Evidence is what you do.

Claims are what you say.

High evidence over claims means high trust.

Low evidence over claims means low trust.

Trust is not binary.

It is a ratio.

The ratio shifts with every action.

A group with high FOT coordinates without hierarchy.

A group with low FOT needs rules and enforcement.

The goal is to raise the FOT.

Raise it by producing evidence.

Reduce it by making claims without evidence.

## The Thirteen Sacred Enfoldments

The thirteen Sacred Enfoldments describe stages of group maturity.

Each enfoldment deepens the FOT.

Early enfoldments are shared space and shared food.

Middle enfoldments are shared work and shared risk.

Late enfoldments are shared identity and shared legacy.

The progression is not mandatory.

Groups move at their own pace.

Some stay in early enfoldments.

This is fine.

The enfoldments describe a range.
