## Context-Dependent Replacements

### "Leverage" Replacements
- When meaning "to use": use, apply, employ
- When meaning "to take advantage of": use, apply
- When meaning "to amplify": increase, boost, enhance

### "Optimize" Replacements
- When meaning "to improve": improve, enhance
- When meaning "to make more effective": make more effective, increase efficiency
- When meaning "to perfect": perfect, refine

### "Holistic" Replacements
- When meaning "complete": complete, full, total
- When meaning "comprehensive": comprehensive, thorough
- When meaning "integrated": integrated, unified, combined

### "Synergy" Replacements
- When meaning "combined effect": combined effect, combined action
- When meaning "cooperation": cooperation, teamwork, collaboration
- When meaning "interaction": interaction, relationship

## Usage Rules

1. Always prefer the shortest approved alternative
2. Use context to choose the most appropriate alternative
3. Never use non-approved words in titles or headings
4. In body text, replace with approved alternatives
5. If an approved word is insufficient, use the least problematic alternative and note it

## Important Notes

- "Utilize" is approved ONLY when "use" does not convey the same meaning
- Metaphor usage in body text requires an introduction sentence within 5 words of first use
- Title fields must never contain metaphors unless explicitly named as such
