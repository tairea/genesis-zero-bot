# ASD-STE100 Word and Sentence Limits

## Sentence Limits

| Sentence Type | Maximum Words | Example |
|--------------|---------------|---------|
| Procedural sentence | 20 words | "Turn the valve clockwise until it stops." |
| Descriptive sentence | 25 words | "The tank holds 100 liters of water at normal pressure." |
| Safety instruction | 20 words | "WARNING: High voltage. Disconnect power before opening." |

## Noun Cluster Limits

- Maximum 3 words per noun cluster
- Compound nouns must be approved forms
- Hyphenated compounds count as single words

## Paragraph Limits

- Maximum 6 sentences per paragraph
- Recommended: 3-4 sentences per paragraph
- One idea per paragraph

## Document Structure

- Section titles: Maximum 40 characters
- List items: Maximum 20 words
- Table cells: Maximum 25 words
- Figure captions: Maximum 25 words

## Writing Level

- Target reading level: Grade 8 (elementary)
- Average sentence length: 12-15 words
- Word selection: Common, everyday English
- Avoid complex grammatical structures

## Technical Writing Specifics

### Procedure Format
```
1. Step one (imperative verb).
2. Step two (imperative verb).
3. Step three (imperative verb).
```

### Description Format
```
Subject + specific detail + measurement or specification.
```

### Warning Format
```
WARNING: [Hazard] + [Consequence] + [Preventive Action].
```
