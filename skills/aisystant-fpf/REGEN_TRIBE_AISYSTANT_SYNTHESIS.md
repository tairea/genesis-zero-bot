SECTION 8: KEY GOVERNANCE PROTOCOLS — FORMALIZED

PROTOCOL 1: WHY-CARDS (Y-CARDS)
  U.Signature (FPF): "I declare I heard this concern before acting"
    Boundary statement: concern brought to community awareness
  U.Commitment (FPF): Deontic obligation to hear before acting
    "I will not act on X until I have heard concerns from those affected"
  AISYSTANT:
    Role: Community Member
    Method: Why-Card protocol (raise concern → wait → hear → act)
    Work Artifact: Recorded concern + acknowledgment + response
  FOT Connection: Affects FOT Indicator 5 (Psychological Safety)
    Raises: Members feel safe to raise concerns without retaliation

PROTOCOL 2: TIME-LOCK (30-DAY)
  U.Signature: "I commit to waiting 30 days before final decision"
    Boundary statement: time boundary on impulsive or captured decisions
  U.Commitment: Deontic obligation to not finalize within window
    "I will not finalize X decision until 30 days have passed"
  AISYSTANT:
    Role: Decision Maker (any role making significant decisions)
    Method: 30-day wait + review at day 15 and day 30
    Work Artifact: Time-stamped decision record with mid-point review
  FOT Connection: Affects FOT Indicator 1 (Mutual Support Frequency)
    Raises: Prevents snap judgments that undermine spontaneous support

PROTOCOL 3: BARN RAISING
  U.Signature: "I will contribute until shared success is achieved"
    Boundary statement: contribution until threshold reached
  U.Commitment: Deontic obligation to continue contributing
    "I commit N hours/resources until this project succeeds"
  AISYSTANT:
    Role: Contributor (any community member)
    Method: Barn raising (contribute until shared success)
    Work Artifact: Logged contributions + shared success metric
  FOT Connection: Affects FOT Indicator 2 (Response Velocity)
