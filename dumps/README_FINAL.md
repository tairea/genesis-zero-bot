## Four Distinctions

The system separates all user input into four categories. This separation is mandatory. No mixing.

### Need

Definition: Personal requirements for survival and thriving.

Relationship: You HAVE needs. You can satisfy needs alone.

Example: I need food, safety, belonging.

### Belief

Definition: Claims about what is true.

Relationship: You THINK beliefs. You hold beliefs in isolation.

Example: I believe the earth is warming.

### Principle

Definition: Rules you follow for behavior.

Relationship: You FOLLOW principles. You follow them even when unobserved.

Example: I never lie to protect myself.

### Value

Definition: What you practice with others.

Relationship: You LIVE values. Values require another being.

Example: I practice generosity with my neighbors.
