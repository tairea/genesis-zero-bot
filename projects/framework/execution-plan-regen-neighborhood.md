## Overview

This plan blends 4 frameworks into a coherent journey:

| Framework | Role |
|-----------|------|

## Phase 0: Foundation (Months 0-2)
*Parallel: CLIPS Individual + Community Layers*

### Goals
- Form core founding group (3-7 people)
- Assess individual readiness
- Build trust + relationships
- Define shared intention

### Tools & Actions

| Week | Action | Framework |
|------|--------|-----------|
| 1-2 | **Individual Assessment**: Each founder reflects on "Why am I here? What am I bringing? What do I need?" | CLIPS Individual |
| 2-4 | **Community Discovery**: Group meets 3+ times to experience dynamics. Use CLIPS "Getting to Know Each Other" methods | CLIPS Community |
| 4-6 | **Intention Setting**: Co-create founding vision + purpose. Document "Why this, Why now" | CLIPS Intention + Alchemy Step 1 |
| 6-8 | **Group Agreements**: Draft working agreements, communication norms, decision-making approach | CLIPS Structure + Alchemy Step 3 |

### Health Check
Use CLIPS self-assessment tool at end of Phase 0. Rate:
- Individual readiness (1-10)
- Community trust level (1-10)
- Intention clarity (1-10)

**Gate:** Minimum 7/10 on all before proceeding.

## Phase 1: Vision to Plan (Months 2-6)
*Parallel: Alchemy Steps 1-4 + RN Spiral 1 + CLIPS Intention*

### Goals
- Complete community vision
- Recruit expanded membership (10-20)
- Define governance model
- Identify land + funding strategy

### Tools & Actions

| Week | Action | Framework |
|------|--------|-----------|
| 9-10 | **Hone Community Vision** (deepen from Phase 0) | Alchemy Step 1 |
| 10-12 | **Recruit Members**: Define membership criteria, outreach strategy | Alchemy Step 2 |
| 12-14 | **Group Agreements & Governance**: Choose model (Sociocracy, Holacracy, DAO, etc.) | Alchemy Step 3 + RCOS Layer 2 |
| 14-16 | **Design Community & Income Models**: Economic system, revenue streams, cost sharing | Alchemy Step 4 + RCOS Layer 3 |
| 16-18 | **Local Connection**: Visit area, introduce yourself to neighbors, attend local events, listen to community history and needs | Alchemy Step 5 + RCOS Layer 1 |
| 18-20 | **Acquire Land**: Site selection, legal structure, purchase/lease | Alchemy Step 5 |
| 20-24 | **Identify Funding Needs**: Capital requirements, grants, investors, community financing | Alchemy Step 6 |

### Milestone (RN Spiral 1: Proof-of-Concept)

- [ ] Core group: 5+ committed members
- [ ] Governance model selected
- [ ] **Local neighbors connected**: Met neighbors, attended local events, understand community context

- [ ] Funding roadmap drafted
