# FOUNDATIONAL RESEARCH

## UN Frameworks

### UN Transparency Protocol (UNTP)

- Architecture: Decentralized supply chain traceability
- Components: Product data, facility data, traceability data, conformity data, identity data
- Digital Product Passports: Carriers of sustainability claims
- 2026: Central EU registry launch (July)
- 2030 Target: 1M product passports/day
- Compliance: Anyone exporting to EU needs this

### EU Digital Product Passport (DPP)

- Mandatory: 2027+ for EV batteries, textiles; 2030 for all
- Data: Origin, materials, sustainability, carbon footprint
- Failure: Product blocking from EU market
- Market opportunity: 50,000+ companies need compliance
