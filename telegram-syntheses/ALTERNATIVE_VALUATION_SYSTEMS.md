## METHOD 2: MUTUAL CREDIT

### How It Works

**Key difference from time banking:** Different labor = different value, but no interest.

| Feature | Bank Credit | Mutual Credit |
|---------|------------|--------------|
| Interest | 5-30% | 0% |
| Approval | Bank decides | Network decides |
| Limit | Credit score | Reputation |
| Risk | 1 bank | All members |

### Formula

```
Your commitment (labor/goods) = Your credit
Your credit = Their debt to network
Your debt = Your commitment to fulfill
Net = siempre zero
```
