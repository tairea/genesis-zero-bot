# UN Transparency Ecosystem + RBTP Overlay Analysis
**Compiled:** 2026-05-02 | **Sources:** UNTP, RBTP, GTR, RBA, TextileGenesis, ISO

## New Entities Mapped

### UNTP (United Nations Transparency Protocol)
- **Source:** untp.unece.org (UNECE)
- **Purpose:** Combat greenwashing via supply chain traceability
- **Functions:** Digitally verifiable ESG compliance, supply chain due diligence, export market access
- **Built on:** UNTP Core Vocabulary (shared credential classes)

### RBTP (Responsible Business Transparency Protocol)
- **Source:** docs.rbtp.pyx.io (RBA)
- **RBA Members:** Apple, Dell, Samsung, Intel, etc. (world's largest industry coalition)
- **Code:** 5 areas - Labor, Health/Safety, Environment, Ethics, Management Systems
- **Key Tools:** VAP auditing, CSR Boot Camp, Responsible Recruitment Due Diligence Toolkit
- **Initiatives:** RMI (Minerals), RLI (Labor), RFI (Factory), REI (Environment)

### GTR (Global Trust Registry)
- **Source:** un.opensource.unicc.org/unece/uncefact/gtr
- **Purpose:** Global Registrar Information Directory + Digital Identity Anchor (DIA)
- **3 phases:** Understand → Explore → Define recommendations
- **DIA:** Verifiable credential for authoritative registers

### EU Digital Product Passport (DPP)
- **Launch:** July 2026 (EU Registry)
- **Applies to:** Nearly all physical products in EU; priority: batteries, textiles, electronics
- **Built on:** UNTP Core Vocabulary, UN/CEFACT Product Circularity Data Standard

### TextileGenesis
- Commercial SaaS platform for textile traceability
- FiberCoin™ proprietary token system
- 2B+ units tracked, 150 brands, 14,000 users, 65+ countries
- 90% of major sustainable material standards integrated
- EU DPP compliant

### ISO/TC 307
- Global blockchain standards: ISO 9001, ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 27701, ISO 31000, ISO 37301, ISO/IEC 42001

## 10-Actor Coordination Map

1. **BlackRock** - financial layer (private markets capture)
2. **Palantir** - data layer (AIP ontology)
3. **Integral** - economic design (post-monetary commons)
4. **Idoneus** - tokenization (captured luxury niche)
5. **RWA ecosystem** - tokenization infrastructure (ERC-3643/1400/1404)
6. **UNTP** - transparency protocol (global supply chain)
7. **RBTP/RBA** - industry compliance (top-down audit model)
8. **GTR** - trust registry (identity anchors)
9. **EU DPP** - regulatory mandate (July 2026)
10. **TextileGenesis** - commercial traceability

## Key Conflicts

| Dimension | RBTP/RBA | Integral |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Structure | Top-down industry coalition | Bottom-up federated commons |
| Model | Audit/compliance hierarchy | Consensus-based governance |
| Membership | Brand-dominant | Node-dominant |
| Power | Brands over suppliers | Cooperative |

## Key Alignments

- UNTP + RWA: traceable tokens for supply chain ownership
- Integral + UNTP: knowledge commons + product provenance
- Palantir + GTR: semantic layer + identity layer (both solve interoperability)
- BlackRock + EU DPP: tokenized assets require DPP compliance
