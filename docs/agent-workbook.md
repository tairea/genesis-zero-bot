## What is a Regenerative Neighborhood
A Regenerative Neighborhood is an ecologically, socially, culturally, economically sustainable and intentional community land development creating net positive impact in these areas.

## What is a Regenerative Neighborhood Experience
A temporary living place for visitors to experience regenerative neighborhood community life.

Both are important for the growth of the Regenerative Neighborhood movement!

---

## Introduction to Regen Tribe

The Regen Tribe Collective designs tools and offers services that create these community land developments at village scale (20-200 people) up to microcity scale (10,000-50,000) with self-sufficient water, food, energy, air, and circular waste-resource management systems.

As leaders in the fields of real estate, land development, architecture, and sustainable construction turn to the creation of these regenerative neighborhoods, Regen Tribe will be there with:

- Well-researched best practices
- The full suite online platform to identify early projects of interest and funnel them through our accelerator
- An end to end consulting agency and network of verified service providers to create these projects and ensure they are successful long-term
- An AI-powered SaaS product that indexes existing knowledge and gives custom support for the creation and management of regenerative neighborhoods

## Community Alchemy Guide Questions

### Section 0: Basic Info

1. **Living option** (Select multiple)
   - Regenerative neighborhood experience (short/mid-term rentals, co.living experiences)
   - Regenerative Neighborhoods (buy/steward land/house, long-term/permanent living)

2. **Status** — Stage of neighborhood functionality:
   - We have a plan - no land yet
   - We have land - no construction yet
   - We are under construction - No one living on site yet
   - We are under construction - Community living on site

5. **State**
6. **City**
7. **Address** (relative location if keeping private)
